Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences over time.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure site security and prevent fraud (necessary cookies).
  • Analyze site usage and improve performance (analytics cookies).
