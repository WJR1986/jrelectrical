About Us

Hello, I'm Jay Robinson, and I'm proud to be your local, qualified electrician serving Alnwick, Northumberland, and the surrounding areas. At J. Robinson Electrical Contractor, my commitment is simple: to provide reliable, safe, and high-quality electrical services for both homes and businesses.

Whether it's a small repair, a new installation, or essential electrical testing, I approach every job with meticulous attention to detail and a focus on efficiency. I believe in clear, straightforward communication, ensuring you always know what's happening and can have complete peace of mind with your electrical work.

Your safety is my absolute priority. As a fully qualified and insured electrical contractor, I adhere strictly to all current UK electrical regulations and industry standards. You can trust J. Robinson Electrical Contractor to deliver professional and dependable results every time, ensuring your electrical systems are safe and sound.

Our Electrical Services

Comprehensive and reliable electrical solutions for homes and businesses in Alnwick & Northumberland.

New Installations & Rewiring

From full property rewires to new circuit installations for extensions or renovations, we ensure safe and compliant electrical systems for your home or business.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Modernise your electrical system by upgrading old fuse boxes to new, compliant consumer units, offering enhanced safety, RCD protection, and peace of mind.

Fault Finding & Repairs

Experiencing tripped circuits or power issues? We quickly diagnose and repair electrical faults, ensuring your property's safety and restoring power efficiently.

Lighting & Power Solutions

Installation of internal and external lighting, additional sockets, and switches. We cover everything from energy-efficient LEDs to smart home integrations.

Electrical Safety Certificates (EICR)

Mandatory or routine Electrical Installation Condition Reports (EICR) for landlords and homeowners, ensuring your wiring meets current UK safety standards.

Outdoor & Garden Electrics

Safe installation of exterior lighting, power sockets, and wiring for sheds, garages, and outdoor living spaces, all compliant with outdoor regulations.
